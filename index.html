Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about sourcing with CropCart.

What areas do you serve?

We currently operate across Greater Accra and surrounding regions. Our network includes farms within a 150km radius to ensure freshness and reduce transit time.

What's your minimum order size?

For wholesale buyers, we require a minimum of 10 mixed cases per delivery. Restaurants and smaller operations can build custom boxes starting from 5 cases.

How do you ensure product quality?

Every farm partner is audited quarterly. We track cold-chain compliance, use GPS-enabled reefer trucks, and provide real-time quality alerts. All produce is graded at source.

Can I customize my delivery schedule?

Absolutely. Set up recurring orders on your preferred days or place one-time orders with 48-hour notice. We offer morning and afternoon delivery windows.

What payment terms do you offer?

We accept Mobile Money, bank transfers, and invoice with net-30 terms for established accounts. All transactions are secured and transparent through our platform.
