How CropCart GH Works

From harvest to handoff in under 12 hours. Our sealed cold-chain and farm-direct model ensures the freshest produce reaches your kitchen with full traceability.

The Process

1

Place Your Order

Browse our catalog of Ghana-grown produce. Set your delivery schedule and choose from verified farms within 200km of Accra.

2

Farm Verification

Our team verifies availability, quality standards, and cold-chain readiness at partner farms. Every item is graded at source.

3

Harvest & Pack

Produce is harvested at peak ripeness in the early morning, hand-sorted, and packed into GPS-tracked containers with temperature monitors.

4

Cold-Chain Transit

Reefer trucks with live temperature monitoring maintain 2-4?C from farm gate to your door. Real-time GPS tracking available via dashboard.

5

Quality Check

Before delivery, produce undergoes final quality inspection. Any items below standard are rejected and farms are notified.

6

Delivery

Produce arrives at your location within 12 hours of harvest. Scheduled morning or afternoon windows, contactless handoff available.

A Typical Day

3
1

4:00 AM Dawn Harvest

Farms begin harvesting greens, tomatoes, and delicate produce at dawn when temperatures are cool and moisture content is optimal.

2

6:00 AM Sorting & Packing

Produce is hand-sorted, graded, and packed into refrigerated containers. Temperature monitors are activated and GPS trackers attached.

3

7:30 AM Dispatch

Reefer trucks depart from farm consolidation points. Cold-chain begins with target 2-4?C maintained throughout transit.

4

10:00 AM Quality Check

Shipments arrive at our Accra hub for final quality inspection and load verification before final-mile delivery.

5

11:00 AM Delivery

Morning delivery window begins. Produce reaches restaurants and wholesalers within 7-12 hours of harvest.

6

3:00 PM Afternoon Window

Second delivery window for businesses that prefer afternoon restocking. Same cold-chain standards maintained.
